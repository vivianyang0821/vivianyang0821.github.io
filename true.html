Monte Carlo Markov Chain

By assigning an offense score and a defense score to each team, we model the number of goals a team scores in a match as a Poisson distribution on with a time discount factor. The mean of the Poisson distribution corresponding to a match equals the ratio of the offense score of the team to the defense score of the opponent. We then sample the offense score and the defense score of each team iteratively using Metropolis Hastings sampling with normal distributions as proposal distributions. The ranking is based on the offense and defense a team.
